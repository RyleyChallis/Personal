Elate Coffee

About the Business

Elate is an artisanal coffee roastery based in Brighton. They prioritize sustainable sourcing and a rhythmic, coastal lifestyle. The digital goal was to translate this sensory experience into a high-performance web interface.

Technical Toolkit

  • Vanilla JavaScript
  • Semantic HTML5
  • SCSS Architecture
  • Figma (UI/UX)
  • GSAP Animations

The Outcome

A 98/100 mobile performance score and a 20% increase in digital table bookings within the first month of launch.
