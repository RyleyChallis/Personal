Elate Coffee

Year2024
RoleFull Stack Dev
StackVanilla JS / SCSS
01 / The Brief

Elate is a Brighton-based roastery that needed a digital home reflecting their "Coastal Soul" philosophy. They required more than a menu; they needed an immersive experience that converted casual browsers into loyal subscribers.

The challenge was to balance high-resolution atmospheric photography with lightning-fast load speeds, ensuring the artisanal feel wasn't compromised by technical lag.

02 / Execution

Performance-First Engineering

Custom Core

Built without bulky frameworks to ensure a 99/100 performance score on mobile devices.

Fluid UI

Implemented custom CSS transitions that mimic the slow, rhythmic flow of pouring coffee.
