02 // TIPSY KISSES

Tipsy Kisses is a mobile bar based in Brighton, specialising in bespoke cocktails for events. I was tasked with creating a high-performance website that not only showcased their unique offerings but also provided a seamless user experience across all devices.

By implementing a clean, modern design and optimising load times, I ensured that visitors could easily navigate the site and access information about their services, menus, and contact details without any friction.

03 // ELATE

Elate is an artisan coffee and kitchen destination that prides itself on the intersection of coastal soul and culinary precision. The project required a digital identity that mirrored the tactile, handcrafted nature of their offerings. I was tasked with creating a visual-first platform that communicates the quality of their locally-sourced ingredients while maintaining a minimalist, sophisticated aesthetic that appeals to the refined Hove community.

To reflect Elate’s commitment to excellence, I built a lightweight, ultra-responsive interface that eliminates all technical friction. By optimizing image delivery and refining the site’s mobile navigation, I ensured that the digital transition from social discovery to the "Book a Table" call-to-action is instantaneous. The resulting site acts as a silent extension of their physical space—fast, clean, and meticulously crafted.
